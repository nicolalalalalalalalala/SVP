Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026.

ShoreVest Partners, Ltd. and relevant affiliates (collectively, "ShoreVest", "we", "us", or "our") process personal information in connection with our website and related business communications. This page describes how we handle that information in a business-to-business context.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information processed through:

  • our public website;
  • restricted investor-access areas linked from the website; and
  • related communications arising from those channels.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites or services that maintain separate privacy notices, and does not replace confidentiality or data provisions in fund, investor, employment, or services documentation.

2. Personal information we collect

Depending on your interaction with us, we may collect:

  • Professional and contact details - such as name, employer, role, business email address, and business telephone number.
  • Enquiry and communications data - such as messages, meeting requests, and correspondence records.
  • Investor-access information - such as account identifiers, authentication metadata, and eligibility-related information where relevant.
  • Technical and usage data - such as IP address, browser and device information, pages viewed, timestamps, and referring URLs.
  • Compliance-related information - where required for legal or regulatory checks connected to investor access or business relationships.
  • Recruitment information - where you submit an application through our website or linked channels.

3. Sources of personal information

We collect personal information:

  • directly from you;
  • automatically through website technologies;
  • from your employer, representatives, advisers, and service providers involved in relevant interactions; and
  • from publicly available and compliance-screening sources where appropriate.

4. How we use personal information

We use personal information for legitimate business and compliance purposes, including to:

  • operate, secure, and maintain our website and investor-access functionality;
  • respond to enquiries and manage professional relationships;
  • assess and administer investor-access requests;
  • send business-related updates where appropriate;
  • conduct security monitoring, diagnostics, and internal reporting;
  • meet legal, regulatory, and record-keeping obligations; and
  • establish, exercise, or defend legal rights.

5. Legal basis - where applicable

Where a legal basis is required, ShoreVest relies on one or more of the following as applicable: legitimate interests, performance of contract or pre-contractual steps, compliance with legal obligations, and consent where specifically required by law.

6. How we disclose personal information

We may disclose personal information on a need-to-know basis to:

  • ShoreVest affiliates;
  • technology, hosting, portal, compliance, and professional service providers;
  • legal, regulatory, governmental, and law-enforcement authorities where required or appropriate; and
  • relevant counterparties and advisers in connection with corporate or fund-related activity.

We do not disclose personal information to data brokers. We do not use personal information collected through this website for mass-market consumer advertising.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, maintain security, and understand aggregate usage patterns.

Where required by law, non-essential cookies are deployed only with appropriate consent controls. For further information, please refer to our Cookie Notice.

8. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including legal, regulatory, accounting, and dispute-resolution requirements. Retention periods vary by data type and context.

9. Data security

We apply technical and organisational measures designed to protect personal information against unauthorised access, misuse, loss, or alteration. No system is entirely risk-free.

10. International transfers

Given the cross-border nature of our business, personal information may be processed outside your jurisdiction. Where required by applicable law, we apply recognised transfer safeguards.

11. Your rights - where applicable

Depending on your jurisdiction and relationship with ShoreVest, you may have rights in relation to your personal information, including rights of access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, and complaint to a regulator. These rights are not absolute and may be subject to legal or regulatory limitations.

To make a request, contact us at privacy@shorevest.com. We may take reasonable steps to verify your identity before acting on a request.

12. Children

This website is intended for professional and institutional audiences and is not directed to children.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any update will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

14. Contact

For privacy enquiries, contact privacy@shorevest.com.

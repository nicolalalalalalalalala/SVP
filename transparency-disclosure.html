Transparency & Disclosure

Our approach to clear communication and responsible disclosure.

Last Updated: June 2026

Disclosure Principles

  • Communications should be fair, balanced, and not misleading.
  • Material assumptions, risks, and limitations should accompany return or opportunity discussions.
  • Historical information should be clearly distinguished from forward-looking expectations.

Performance and Transaction References

Any references to historical transactions, case studies, market observations, or performance outcomes are provided for informational purposes only. Past performance is not necessarily indicative of future results, and outcomes may differ significantly across structures, vintage periods, and market environments.

Conflicts and Governance

ShoreVest Partners maintains governance procedures designed to identify and manage potential conflicts of interest, including review protocols, escalation processes, and controls aligned with applicable fiduciary and regulatory obligations.

Third-Party Data and Sources

Materials may include data derived from third-party sources believed to be reliable. Such information may not be independently verified by ShoreVest Partners, and no representation is made as to its completeness or accuracy.

Investor Reporting

Fund-specific disclosures and reports are provided to existing investors through designated channels and subject to applicable confidentiality and legal restrictions. For investor materials, contact ir@shorevest.com.

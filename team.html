Team

ShoreVest Team

In a market where outcomes are determined by local relationships, legal precision, and the ability to enforce, the quality of the team is the strategy. ShoreVest’s professionals span investment underwriting, legal structuring, creditor negotiation, asset resolution, and investor coverage — built over two decades of operating through every phase of China’s credit cycle.

Leadership and Investment

The leadership and investment team drives portfolio construction, underwriting discipline, and investment decision-making across ShoreVest’s core private credit strategy.

Benjamin Fanger
Managing Partner & Founder

Benjamin Fanger co-founded Shoreline Capital in 2004, one of China's earliest distressed debt platforms. He later built ShoreVest's current platform and investment strategy, and he has worked in China's credit markets for more than 20 years. He is based in Guangzhou.

Ada Bi
Partner, CFO

Ada Bi leads financial management, fund operations, and LP reporting across ShoreVest's platform. She brings years of experience in alternative asset fund operations and institutional financial controls.

Hui Zheng

Mike Chang

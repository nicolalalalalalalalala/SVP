# Quantifying China’s NPL Market

**Series:** China Debt Dynamics
**Volume / Issue:** Volume 8 | Issue 2
**Date:** Q2 2024

A framework for sizing and segmenting China’s non-performing-loan opportunity set.

This issue is presented in ShoreVest’s unified print format for consistency across the China Debt Dynamics archive.

## Key Findings

- NPL inventories remain heterogeneous across regions and lender types.
- Resolution outcomes are driven by collateral quality, process speed, and local execution.
- Specialist sourcing and restructuring capacity are key to realized returns.

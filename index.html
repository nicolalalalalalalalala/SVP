HOW WE WORK

VISION & PRINCIPLES

ShoreVest’s vision is to deliver consistent returns across cycles in China’s private credit market by sourcing proprietary opportunities the broader market cannot reach, applying disciplined analysis with first-lien downside protection, and maintaining alignment of interests with investors.

At its core, this is a human business, built on relationships of trust with the networks that originate opportunities and the institutions that commit capital to the platform.

INVESTMENT STRATEGY

WHERE CONVENTIONAL CREDIT FAILS, COLLATERAL AND PROCESS DEFINE RECOVERY.

ShoreVest invests in asset-backed private credit situations where low loan-to-value or cost-to-value provides a meaningful margin of safety, and where local sourcing, legal process, and operational capability can unlock returns that conventional lenders cannot access.

01

Asset-Based Lending

First-lien, asset-backed financing to well-performing companies within China's private credit market.

02

Asset Restructuring

Credit clean-up situations involving good underlying assets and challenged balance sheets, including select distressed debt.

03

Debt Resolutions

Working with financial institutions seeking to divest distressed portfolios, including non-performing loans (NPLs).

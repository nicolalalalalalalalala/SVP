HOW WE WORK

Vision & Principles

ShoreVest’s vision is to deliver consistent returns across cycles in China’s credit markets through a long-term platform with the local capability to source, analyze, and execute with discipline.

The firm’s approach is grounded in trust: with the networks that originate opportunities, and with the investors who commit capital to the platform.

01

Proprietary Sourcing

Originate opportunities through longstanding onshore networks and local execution channels not broadly intermediated in market processes.

02

Disciplined Analysis

Underwrite to protected downside, enforceable collateral and practical work-out pathways before capital is committed.

03

Alignment of Interests

Maintain structural and governance alignment with investors through the full investment lifecycle, from origination to exit.

STRATEGY

Investment Strategy

ShoreVest is a multi-strategy private credit platform specializing in asset-backed opportunities in China.

Across each strategy, the common discipline is the same: conservative entry points, downside protection, and a focus on situations where local execution can create value.

Rather than relying on documentation alone, the strategy is built around collateral control, enforceable structures, and the ability to manage underwriting, servicing, and recovery through one integrated platform.

01
ASSET-BASED LENDING
Providing first-lien, asset-backed financing to operating businesses with defensible collateral and visible cash flow.
02
ASSET RESTRUCTURING
Executing credit clean-up and recapitalization situations where strong underlying assets are burdened by stressed balance sheets.
03
DEBT RESOLUTIONS
Partnering with financial institutions to resolve and divest distressed portfolios, including non-performing loans.

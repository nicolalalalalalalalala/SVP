ShoreVest Partners, 新岸资本, is a specialist private credit manager focused exclusively on China's debt markets. The strategy traces back to 2004, and the current platform was established in 2016 by the senior team that built it and continues to run it under the same underwriting discipline. We have deployed more than US$2 billion across six fund vintages, navigating every phase of China's credit cycle with no principal losses at the individual deal level to date. Our approach centers on asset-backed collateral, conservative entry points, and directly enforceable positioning under PRC law, with return drivers tied primarily to idiosyncratic, asset-level outcomes rather than global liquid credit beta. This focus is intended to provide a structurally low-correlation source of credit exposure within broader institutional portfolios.

About the Firm

How we work.

Our process starts with local sourcing pipelines and rigorous underwriting: collateral-first screening, legal-enforceability checks under PRC law, and downside cases built before capital is committed.

The firm focuses exclusively on asset-backed private credit in China: completed, marketable, cash-flowing collateral with conservative entry points and multiple exit routes. This is a market that rewards local presence, legal precision, and the practical ability to enforce — and punishes those without it.

Proprietary Sourcing
Direct relationships with banks, AMCs, law firms, and servicers — built over two decades. Access to opportunities before competitive auction, with the ability to source bilaterally or select from larger portfolios.
Disciplined Underwriting
Every transaction anchored to hard-asset collateral, conservative loan-to-value or cost-to-value thresholds (typically 30–60%), and first-lien or otherwise directly enforceable positioning. Downside defined before upside.

The same team that approves investments remains accountable for monitoring and exit, with clear ownership of results throughout the investment lifecycle.

What We Do

Three strategies.
One discipline.

Each strategy is underpinned by asset-backed collateral, conservative entry points, and senior or directly enforceable positioning. Allocation shifts based on relative opportunity.

Strategy 01
First-Lien Asset-Based Lending
Senior-secured financing to performing borrowers when traditional bank lending is constrained. Every loan backed by hard assets — primarily real estate — with conservative LTVs and first-lien priority.
Strategy 02
Asset Restructuring & Workouts
Complex situations where the underlying asset is sound but the capital structure is not. Active investments requiring creditor coordination, balance-sheet redesign, and often operational control before exit. Two decades of pattern recognition across China's restructuring cycles.
Strategy 03
Debt Resolutions & Recoveries
Acquisition and resolution of non-performing loans from banks and financial institutions under pressure to divest. Sourcing, diligence, legal enforcement, and disposition managed in-house through ShoreVest Asset Solutions.

ABOUT THE FIRM

A firm rooted
in two worlds.

ShoreVest is a specialist private credit platform focused on asset-backed opportunities in China. Since 2004, the firm has invested across lending, restructurings, and debt resolutions, with a long-standing team based onshore and a platform designed for institutional capital.

HOW WE WORK

Vision &
Principles.

ShoreVest’s vision is to deliver consistent returns across cycles in China’s credit markets through a long-term platform with the local capability to source, analyze, and execute with discipline.

The firm’s approach is grounded in trust: with the networks that originate opportunities, and with the investors who commit capital to the platform.

Onshore in execution. Institutional in discipline.

PROPRIETARY SOURCING
Source proprietary opportunities, information, and exit channels not broadly available to the market.

STRATEGY

Investment
Strategy.

ShoreVest is a multi-strategy private credit platform specializing in asset-backed opportunities in China. Across each strategy, the common discipline is the same: conservative entry points, downside protection, and a focus on situations where local execution can create value.

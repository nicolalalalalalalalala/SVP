2. What are cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow a website to recognise your device and store information about your interaction with the Site.

In addition to cookies, ShoreVest may use similar technologies, including:

  • local storage objects and session storage;
  • web beacons, pixels, and tracking tags;
  • device identifiers; and
  • server-side logging and tracking mechanisms.

These technologies may operate independently or in conjunction with cookies.

For the purposes of this Notice, all such technologies are referred to collectively as “cookies”.

Cookie Notice

Effective date: April 8, 2026.

This Cookie Notice explains how ShoreVest uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites operate, improve performance, and remember certain settings.

2. Cookies we use

We use the following categories of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies - required for core website operation, security, and network management.
  • Performance and analytics cookies - used to understand aggregate traffic and improve website performance.
  • Preference cookies - used to remember selected settings where applicable.

3. Legal basis and consent

Where required by applicable law, non-essential cookies are used only with appropriate consent controls. Strictly necessary cookies are used as needed to operate and secure the website.

4. How to manage cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, including deleting existing cookies and blocking future cookies. If you block certain cookies, some website functions may not operate as intended.

5. Third-party technologies

We may use third-party service providers for analytics, hosting, and security support. Those providers may set or access cookies in connection with their services to ShoreVest.

6. Updates to this Notice

We may update this Cookie Notice from time to time. Any update will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

7. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Notice, contact privacy@shorevest.com.

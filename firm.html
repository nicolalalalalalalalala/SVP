The Firm

ShoreVest is a specialist private credit platform focused on asset-backed investments in China. Since 2004, the firm has built its approach around enforceable collateral, conservative entry levels, and active control through sourcing, underwriting, servicing, and recovery.

China-focused. Legal-first. Collateral-driven.

Built for downside protection before upside pursuit.

Structured for institutional capital across market cycles.

Built for control in complex credit

ShoreVest invests where legal position, collateral coverage, and execution access can be established at entry.

Asset-based lending

Senior secured financing against identifiable hard-asset collateral with documented enforcement pathways.

Asset restructurings

Recapitalization and restructuring mandates where control rights and collateral priority can be contractually preserved.

Debt resolutions

Distressed and non-performing credit situations requiring coordinated legal process, servicing control, and recovery execution.

Common discipline

Transactions are selected and structured to protect principal under stress conditions, not base-case assumptions.

Underwriting discipline

  • First-lien or directly enforceable claims
  • Hard-asset collateral as the primary protection
  • Typical 30–60% LTV or CTV at entry
  • Downside protection before upside pursuit

Why the platform is built this way

In China private credit, outcomes are determined by enforceable onshore rights and local execution quality, not document form alone.

Execution realities

  • Offshore documents alone do not determine practical control in stressed situations.
  • Onshore enforceability and local legal process govern timing and recoveries.
  • Collateral liquidity must be tested against real disposal pathways, not paper valuation.

Portfolio posture

  • Avoidance of unsecured offshore exposure where claim enforcement is structurally weak.
  • Avoidance of development-backed lending where completion and refinancing risk dominate outcomes.
  • Preference for situations where collateral, legal rights, and process control are aligned from day one.

Local presence, national reach

ShoreVest is headquartered in Guangzhou with a Shanghai office and broader coverage across China’s principal credit markets, including Hong Kong connectivity.

A long-horizon institution

ShoreVest is built to compound judgment over decades, with governance and process designed for continuity across credit cycles.

The coastal redwood is a reminder of operating posture: durable root systems, measured growth, and structural resilience under changing conditions. The objective is a hundred-year institution defined by discipline, not cycle timing.

The Firm

ShoreVest is a specialist China private credit platform built for downside protection, legal clarity, and active execution.

We focus on asset-based lending, restructurings, and debt resolution in situations where enforceability and collateral control are clear. The same senior professionals oversee sourcing, underwriting, servicing, and recovery to maintain full-cycle accountability.

The Firm

ShoreVest focuses exclusively on China private credit, in markets where legal structure, local execution and recoverability determine outcomes.

What it is

The platform focuses on asset-based lending, asset restructuring, and debt resolution with enforceable onshore claims and active risk control from origination through exit.

History

The strategy has operated since 2004. The current ShoreVest platform was formed in 2016 by the senior team behind predecessor vehicles across multiple credit cycles.

Headquarters and core execution teams are based in Guangzhou, with in-market coverage across sourcing, underwriting, servicing, and recovery.

The platform is designed for markets where legal structure, local execution, and recoverability matter as much as yield.

Operating model

Four operating pillars govern origination, underwriting, and portfolio management from entry through exit.

Proprietary Sourcing

Direct relationships with banks, AMCs, law firms, and servicers create access before broad auctions, improving price discipline and structural control.

Disciplined Underwriting

Transactions are anchored to hard-asset collateral with first-lien or otherwise enforceable PRC-law positioning, supported by covenant and enforceability review.

Alignment of Interests

The team that approves an investment remains accountable through monitoring and exit, reducing handoff risk and preserving underwriting discipline.

Active Portfolio Management

Each investment is monitored from origination through exit. If performance deteriorates, ShoreVest can escalate through in-house resolution and PRC court enforcement.

Proprietary sourcing in practice

Relationship density across banks, AMCs, law firms, and servicers gives ShoreVest early access to complex situations before broad auctions.

Platform

Why execution differs in China

Credit outcomes in China are shaped by local legal process, creditor positioning in the capital stack, and the practical ability to control and enforce against underlying assets.

In many developed markets, documentation standards, judicial precedent, and enforcement timelines are comparatively uniform. In China, recoveries are more path-dependent: legal standing, claim structure, stakeholder coordination, and execution quality can materially change outcomes. ShoreVest is therefore built as an onshore operating platform with integrated sourcing, legal structuring, negotiation, and resolution capabilities.

How execution is concentrated.

Compared with a typical offshore fund model, ShoreVest keeps sourcing, legal structuring, investment committee governance, and recovery coordination in-house. In China's credit market, enforceability and debt-resolution execution determine realized value.

Decision protocol

One investment team runs one continuous process from origination to exit: Underwrite → Approve → Execute → Monitor + Realize.

Originating + Underwriting

The same partners source, diligence, and structure each deal with downside protection before approval.

Approval + Structuring

Investment, legal, and asset terms are approved in one forum to keep documents and risk controls aligned.

Execution Continuity

The team approving deployment stays responsible through closing and debt resolution, with no handoff.

Monitoring + Realization

Post-close monitoring and realization stay with original decision-makers to keep underwriting tied to outcomes.

Institutional Platform

Rooted in China, built for institutional capital

ShoreVest’s strategy dates to 2004, with the current platform established in 2016 by the senior team behind predecessor vehicles across collateral-backed lending, asset restructuring, and NPL resolution.

Headquartered in Guangzhou with additional presence in Shanghai, Hong Kong, and California, the firm keeps investment, legal, servicing, and realization execution coordinated through one onshore operating standard.

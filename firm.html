CHINA PRIVATE CREDIT · ESTABLISHED 2004

The control architecture behind every credit decision.

ShoreVest is a specialist private credit platform focused on asset-backed investments in China. The underwriting model is built around enforceable collateral, conservative entry levels, and full-cycle control from origination through legal resolution.

Operating logic

  • Collateral first
  • Enforceability verified
  • Onshore execution retained
  • Downside before upside

Strategy design for recoverability, not narrative.

ShoreVest focuses on three complementary segments of China private credit. Each segment is selected for claim clarity, collateral coverage, and practical execution pathways under stressed conditions.

Asset-based lending

Senior secured capital against hard collateral with legal claim pathways mapped before commitment.

Asset restructurings

Recapitalisations where claim priority and structural protections can be contractually sustained.

Debt resolutions

Resolution-driven exposures managed through onshore servicing, enforcement, and recoveries execution.

Selected focus

Asset-based lending

Senior secured financings collateralised by identifiable hard assets, underwritten with explicit enforcement routes and conservative structure from inception.

Why this model works in China

In China private credit, outcomes follow enforceable onshore rights, collateral quality, and execution discipline rather than documentation form alone.

  • Practical control depends on local legal standing and enforceable onshore rights once borrower conditions deteriorate.

  • Recovery outcomes vary materially by court process, local implementation, and procedural sequencing.

  • Underwriting is grounded in realistic disposal routes and time-to-cash assumptions rather than mark-to-model projections.

  • The strategy prioritises directly enforceable claims and avoids structures with limited legal remedy alignment.

  • Focus remains on hard collateral and recoverability to reduce dependence on uncertain project cycles.

  • Entry alignment supports downside protection and creates clearer intervention and resolution pathways.

Institution built for long-duration cycles

Durability at ShoreVest is treated as operating design: repeatable standards, stable judgment, and cumulative execution capability across market regimes.

The objective is not short-period optimisation. It is to compound institutional edge over decades through disciplined underwriting, process continuity, and measured risk transfer.

This long-horizon stance informs team structure, decision cadence, and legal execution posture - preserving control through both expansionary and stressed credit environments.

The Firm

ShoreVest Partners.

Institutional private credit platform for complex recoveries across China.

ShoreVest’s strategy dates to 2004, with the current platform established in 2016 by the senior team behind predecessor vehicles across collateral-backed lending, asset restructuring, and NPL resolution.

We focus on enforceable structures, conservative entry basis, and integrated onshore execution from origination through exit.

Operational geography.

ShoreVest is headquartered in Guangzhou, with a satellite office in Shanghai and a tertiary Hong Kong presence supporting regional connectivity. Core investment, legal, servicing, and resolution functions remain coordinated onshore.

Institutional platform design.

The platform is organized around one operating standard: source selectively, underwrite with legal precision, and control execution through realization.

How the platform operates.

Four operating pillars govern origination, underwriting, and portfolio management in one integrated institutional process.

PILLAR 01

Proprietary sourcing

Direct relationships with banks, AMCs, law firms, and servicers create access to complex situations before broad auction processes.

PILLAR 02

Disciplined underwriting

Transactions are structured around hard-asset collateral, conservative entry points, and enforceable claims under PRC law.

PILLAR 03

Alignment of responsibility

The same investment team stays accountable from approval through monitoring and realization, reducing handoff risk.

PILLAR 04

Active portfolio control

Continuous monitoring and integrated workout execution support timing discipline and recovery outcomes across changing cycles.

    Why China requires a different approach.

    Six structural realities shape execution quality and realized value in this market.

    01

    Legal structure is a value driver

    02

    Onshore enforcement determines outcomes

    Recovery timelines and coordination dynamics require local execution through the full investment lifecycle.

    03

    Origination is relationship-led

    Differentiated opportunities are accessed through long-duration local coverage and trusted networks.

    04

    Borrower behavior is cycle-sensitive

    Pattern recognition and local judgment are required to navigate situational complexity and protect downside.

    05

    Active servicing is not optional

    Post-close monitoring and intervention capability are central to preserving value through volatility.

    06

THE FIRM

SHOREVEST PARTNERS

BUILT BETWEEN GLOBAL CAPITAL AND ONSHORE CHINA EXECUTION.

ShoreVest is an institutional private credit platform specialising in asset-backed opportunities in China. The firm sits deliberately between two systems: the governance, reporting and risk discipline expected by global institutional investors, and the local execution capacity required to originate, structure, service and recover credit inside China.

Founded and led by Benjamin Fanger, a US-trained lawyer and long-standing China private credit investor fluent in Chinese, ShoreVest is not a foreign-capital wrapper around outsourced local execution. Its senior investment committee includes Benjamin Fanger, Ada Bi, Mecy Mei and Hui Zheng, who have worked together across China credit cycles for more than 17 years.

The strategy dates to 2004 and was continued through ShoreVest Partners in 2016 by the senior team behind the predecessor Shoreline vehicles. The platform invests across asset-based lending, asset restructuring and debt resolution, with a consistent focus on enforceable collateral, conservative entry basis, legal priority and practical recovery pathways.

Execution is led onshore through a locally embedded platform headquartered in Guangzhou, supported by China-based investment professionals, in-house legal capability and servicing infrastructure built for diligence, structuring, monitoring and enforcement.

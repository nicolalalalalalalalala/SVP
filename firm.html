The Firm

ShoreVest is a specialist China private credit platform built around one idea: strong outcomes come from disciplined structures and local execution.

Our focus is narrow and deliberate—asset-based lending, restructurings, and debt resolution. We stay in segments where legal enforceability and collateral control are clear.

The same senior team oversees sourcing, underwriting, servicing, and recovery. That full-cycle accountability keeps process, risk, and execution aligned from entry through exit.

How we invest.

Our operating model is intentionally simple: source with access, underwrite for downside, and execute actively to protect value.

Proprietary sourcing

Relationship-led origination provides differentiated visibility and improves structure quality before broad market processes begin.

Downside-first underwriting

We prioritise legal enforceability, collateral strength, and conservative basis before considering upside scenarios.

Active asset management

Execution does not stop at closing; we manage each position through servicing, resolution, and monetisation.

Single-team accountability

Decision-makers stay responsible for the assets they approve, creating tight alignment between underwriting and realised outcomes.

Proprietary sourcing

Longstanding relationships with banks, AMCs, law firms, and servicers help us access complex situations early and structure them with greater control.

Where we operate.

Headquartered in Guangzhou with a Shanghai office, ShoreVest runs an integrated onshore process spanning origination, legal structuring, servicing, and recovery.

Local presence is core infrastructure.

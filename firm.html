The Firm

ShoreVest’s China private credit strategy dates to 2004, with the current platform established in 2016 by the senior team behind predecessor vehicles spanning collateral-backed lending, asset restructuring, and NPL resolution.

The firm focuses exclusively on three sub-strategies in China: asset-based lending, asset restructuring, and debt resolutions. Headquarters and core execution teams are based in Guangzhou, with onshore coverage across sourcing, underwriting, servicing, and recovery.

ShoreVest is built for markets where legal structure, local execution, and recoverability matter as much as yield. The platform emphasises enforceable onshore claims, conservative entry basis, and active control from origination through exit.

How the platform operates.

Four operating pillars govern origination, underwriting, and portfolio management from entry through exit.

Proprietary Sourcing

ShoreVest maintains direct relationships with banks, AMCs, law firms, and servicers built over two decades. Because China's market complexity can fragment information, the platform seeks to convert that complexity into sourcing advantage through bilateral origination and selective portfolio acquisition designed to improve price discovery and structure flexibility.

Disciplined Underwriting

ShoreVest anchors every transaction to hard-asset collateral with conservative loan-to-value or cost-to-value entry points of typically 30-60%, and first-lien or otherwise directly enforceable positioning under PRC law. If exit conditions weaken, this structure is designed to preserve control through covenants, enforceability review, and downside-first underwriting before upside assumptions are considered.

Alignment of Interests

The same team that approves investments remains accountable through monitoring and exit. This model is designed to mitigate handoff risk, and it seeks to keep underwriting assumptions, workout decisions, and realization priorities aligned through a single chain of responsibility.

Active Portfolio Management

ShoreVest continuously monitors each investment from origination through exit. If borrower performance deteriorates, the firm can escalate quickly through ShoreVest Asset Solutions, its in-house resolution capability designed to stabilize recovery outcomes. When consensual paths are insufficient, ShoreVest actively enforces creditor rights through the PRC court process to protect value and timing discipline.

Proprietary sourcing in practice

Relationship density across banks, AMCs, law firms, and servicers helps ShoreVest access complex situations before broad auctions, which is designed to improve pricing discipline and structural control.

Where we operate.

Headquarters in Guangzhou. Satellite office in Shanghai. The investment team is based in China — sourcing, diligence, structuring, servicing, and resolution run through an integrated onshore process. Local presence is not a differentiator. It is a prerequisite.

Why China requires a different approach.

China is the world's second-largest credit market, and for global institutional investors, under-allocation can create its own portfolio imbalance. Periods of heightened concern often coincide with the most attractive entry points for properly structured private credit. China's private credit market is complex and shaped by evolving regulation, which is why consistent results depend on local expertise and disciplined on-the-ground execution.

How execution is concentrated.

Compared with a typical offshore fund model, ShoreVest retains sourcing, legal structuring, investment committee governance, and recovery coordination in-house. That concentration reflects the structural reality of China's credit market: enforceability and workout execution determine realised value, and those workstreams cannot be effectively outsourced.

Decision protocol

One investment team runs one continuous process from origination to exit: Underwrite → Approve → Execute → Monitor + Realize.

Originating + Underwriting

The same partners source, diligence, and structure each deal with downside protection before approval.

Approval + Structuring

Investment, legal, and asset terms are approved in one forum to keep documents and risk controls aligned.

Execution Continuity

The team approving deployment stays responsible through closing and workout, with no handoff.

Monitoring + Realization

Post-close monitoring and realization stay with original decision-makers to keep underwriting tied to outcomes.

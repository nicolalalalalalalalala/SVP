Investment Strategy

ShoreVest underwrites to recoverable value, not just headline yield. Across all sub-strategies, each investment is anchored by hard collateral, enforceable legal control, and multiple exit paths to preserve capital through volatile cycles.

The Opportunity

China is the world's second-largest credit market, yet specialist private credit remains underpenetrated relative to developed markets. That imbalance can create attractive entry points for managers with local legal, servicing, and restructuring capabilities.

Market Snapshot
Scale creates repeatable volume; selectivity drives quality.

Track Record and Current Opportunity Set

Recent portfolio outcomes and current deployment conditions continue to support ShoreVest’s core thesis: low probability of capital loss with high deal-level return potential in China private credit.

Platform Highlights

  • 20-year realized track record across 50+ private credit deals with no principal loss on a deal basis.
  • Managed approximately US$2 billion of China-focused private credit capital for global institutional investors.
  • Recent deals averaged high-20% net deal-level IRRs (unlevered), with ~18-month weighted average life and 8–12% annual cash yield.
  • Current market backdrop is viewed as the most attractive deployment window in the firm’s 20-year history.

Legal Environment and Recovery Paths

PRC enforcement is materially more functional than many offshore allocators assume, with improved auction transparency and clearer creditor pathways for properly structured claims.

Tier-1 courts now process secured claims with greater timeline predictability.

Risk Management Framework

Downside management is structural, not discretionary. Portfolio construction is designed to protect principal first and monetize upside through execution.

Underwriting Controls

  • Conservative entry LTV/CTV bands with stress-tested recovery assumptions.
  • Priority claims, enforceability review, and PRC-law documentation standards.
  • No dependence on refinancing to complete value realization.

Portfolio Controls

  • Diversification by borrower, collateral, vintage, and province.
  • Continuous surveillance from origination through disposition.
  • Multiple predefined exits reduce duration and execution risk.

Returns Profile

The strategy targets approximately 10–15% net returns in USD terms without fund-level leverage, with a focus on current cash yield, principal protection, and shorter path-to-liquidity than many private-market alternatives.
